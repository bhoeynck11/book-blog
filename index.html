Book Reviews

Over the course of the next year, I plan to read several new books -- and I do not discriminate against genre. I enjoy mystery and thriller, suspense, non-fiction, and biographies. As I finish each novel, I will post some of my thoughts and feelings about what makes the book great, as well as some aspects that I did not enjoy as much and how I think the book could be improved.

See if I Can Finish 30 Books in 2016

As 2015 was winding down, I found myself having conversations with friends and family about favorite books. I often found myself feeling left out as others gossiped about the newest bestsellers, as I had never read them (or sometimes heard of them). So, with the new year, I have dedicated myself to reading 30, minimum, new books. Check out the blog on this site to see my progress!
